FIELD OF RESEARCH: STRUCTURAL BIOLOGY
I AM CURRENTLY WORKING TO UNDERSTAND THE PEPTIDYL PROLYL CIS/TRANS ISOMERASE (PPI) PROTEIN PINA IN DICTYOSTELIUM DISCOIDEUM, WHICH HAS ORTHOLOGUE IN HUMANS AS PIN1 AND ESS1 IN YEAST.
I AM CURRENTLY COMPARING THE MSA DATA OF ESS1, PIN 1 AND PIN A (PARVULINS). ONE INTERESTING THING OBSERVED IS THAT THE PIN A PROTEIN HAS FHA DOMAIN TO THE N TERMINAL PEPTIDE REGION EXCLUSIVE TO IT.
PPI MEDIATES THE CIS-TRANS ISOMERISATION TO PROLINE OF N TERMINAL PEPTIDE BONDS. PARVILINS LIKE PPI ARE IMPORTANT FOR CELL CYCLE. PREVIOUS STUDIES SHOWS THAT IT PLAYS ROLE IN GENE REGULATION ALONG WITH KINASES TO MEDIATE THE CYCLE.
DICTYOSTELIUM DISCOIDEUM HAS TWO PARVULINS PIN A (268 AA) AND PIN4 (123 AA) BY SEQUENCE ALIGNMENT. PIN A IS A TRUE HOMOLOG OF ESS1 (S CEREVISIAE) AND HUMAN PIN1 BY GENETIC COMPLEMENTATION IN YEAST. I AM TRYING TO PULL DOWN AND PURIFY THE PROTEIN AND DO CIRCULAR DICHROISM FOR FIGURING OUT THE ACTIVITY AND SECONDARY STRUCTURE INFORMATION AND EVENTUALLY GO FOR CYSTALLISATION, SINCE BOTH THE ACTIVITY AND CRYSTAL DATA ARE YET TO BE STUDIED.
I AM CURRENTLY USING ALPHAFOLD2 TO GENERATE AND CHECK WITH SOME MUTATIONS IN THE SEQUENCE AND THE POSSIBLE STRUCTURAL CONSEQUENCES. I HAVE NOTICED THAT IN THE 260 AA NEIGHBOURHOOD A SINGLE RESIDUE MUTATION OF VALINE CAN CAUSE A BETA STRAND PRESENT IN THE LOCALITY IN THE WILD TYPE TO BE LOST. I HAVE YET TO CHECK THE FUNCTIONAL CONSEQUENCES OF IT, AND I AM PLANNING TO VERIFY THE SAME WITH CRYSTAL STRUCTURES LATER IN X RAY DIFFRACTION.
AS IT IS PPI WHICH IS ESSENTIAL FOR GROWTH IN THIS SOCIAL AMEOBA, IT MIGHT GAVE STONG PHENOTYOPE CHANGES WHICH NEED FURTHER INVERSTIGATION THAT I AM PLANNING TO DO.
